Quick hummus recipe

This recipe makes quick, tasty hummus, with no messing. It has been adapted from a number of different recipes that I have read over the years.

Ingredients

1 can (400g) of chick peas (garbanzo beans)
175g of tanini
6 sundried tomatoes
A pinch of cayenne pepper
A dash of olive oil

Instruction

remove the skin of the garlic, and chop coarsely
Remove oil the seeds and stalk from the pepper, and chop coarsely
Add all the ingredients into a food processor
Process all the ingredient into a paste.
If you want a coarse "chunky" hummus, process it for a short time
if you want a smooth hummus, process it for a longer time

For a different flavour you try blending in a small measure of lemon and coriander, chili pepper, lime chipotle, harisse and mint, or spinach and feta cheese.
